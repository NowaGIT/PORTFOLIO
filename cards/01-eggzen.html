EGGZEN

WINNER · PBLDAY 2025

EGGZEN is an innovative sensory tool designed specifically for children, combining ergonomic design with engaging tactile experiences. This award-winning product prioritizes child development through multisensory interaction.

Project Overview

EGGZEN won first place at PBLDAY 2025 for its innovative approach to child sensory development. The product features organic egg-shaped design with multiple textured surfaces to stimulate tactile learning.

Key Features

  • Ergonomic egg-shaped form optimized for child hands
  • Multiple textured surfaces for sensory exploration
  • Non-toxic, durable materials
  • Lightweight and portable design
  • Educational value integrated into play

Methodology

Extensive user research with child development specialists informed every design decision. Prototype testing with focus groups of children aged 3-8 validated the sensory effectiveness and safety of the product.
