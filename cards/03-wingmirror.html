Wing Mirror Design

Maier · 2025

Automotive wing mirror design for Seat Ateca, developed in collaboration with Maier. This project involved CAD modeling in SolidWorks and aerodynamic optimization.

Project Overview

This professional automotive design project involved creating an advanced wing mirror assembly for the Seat Ateca, one of Spain's leading automotive manufacturers' popular SUV models.

Technical Development

  • Advanced CAD modeling using SolidWorks parametric design
  • Aerodynamic optimization for drag reduction
  • Integration with existing Ateca platform architecture
  • Manufacturing-ready design with DFM/DFA analysis
  • Material selection for automotive durability standards

Collaboration

Worked closely with Maier's engineering team to ensure design compliance with automotive industry standards, safety regulations, and manufacturing constraints. Multiple design iterations refined the final form.
