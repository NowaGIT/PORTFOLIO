MTB Carrier Rack

Orbea · 2024

Innovative mountain bike carrier rack designed for Orbea, a leading premium bicycle manufacturer. This product combines functionality with aesthetic integration.

Project Overview

Developed a specialized carrier rack system for Orbea mountain bikes, designed to safely transport bikes while maintaining the aesthetic integrity of their premium product line.

Design Features

  • Universal compatibility with Orbea MTB models
  • Secure bike mounting system with vibration dampening
  • Lightweight aluminum construction
  • Quick-release mechanisms for easy installation
  • Integrated design philosophy matching Orbea branding
  • Weather-resistant finishes

Development Process

Extensive testing with professional mountain bikers ensured the rack meets demands of real-world use. Prototype iterations refined the structural integrity and user experience. Collaboration with Orbea's product team ensured brand consistency.
